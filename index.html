About Fedu

FEDU is a global platform that identifies and funds educational projects; we connect those in need with people excited to fund them and facilitate the transfer of these contributions. We're a platform for the everyday person to contribute to small-scale educational projects and seek to improve lives by helping those with the greatest barriers to education.

crowd-funding platform

Any organization with education at the forefront of their agenda to create change can use our crowd funding platform to fundraise for projects or students ('beneficiaries"). Our contributors are passionate about education.

Large Network

FEDU has built a large network of businesses and individual contributors. People trust FEDU because we have individually qualified every organization bringing projects and beneficiary stories onto our platform to serve impactful education.

About Us

In April of 2009, Chet was volunteering at a religious event for JCNC when he was approached by a gentleman raising money for a temple. Chet asked the man if the money would go directly to helping an individual or cause and the reply was "no." That was the moment a vision was created. Chet wanted to find a way to channel money directly to those in need. He began funding an underprivileged student named Sagar, helping him receive technical vocational training.
